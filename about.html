About Us, the Resturant People within Sylvia's.

Our founder, Syliva W. Tornis, always loved food and one day she just tried making food without a recipe to follow, and bam, she never looked back ever since. While loving being creative with food, she went to college and got a degree in resturant management and focused on culinary as well.

Our Mission

From the beginning we have been driven by a mission of "doing it the right way" and we have succeeded in establishing ourselves as the premier destination in each community we are in. Our friendly, motivated and sincere team has created a lasting relationship with each guest we encounter. You will be sure to recognize us by name as we will you.
